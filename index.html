Mexican-Style Salad

Ingredients 1x 2x 3x ▢1 Red pepper ▢1 Yellow pepper ▢1 Large Red onions, Or 2 small ▢340 g (11 oz) Sweetcorn, Drained ▢200 g (7 oz) Salsa, The fresh kind, from the fridge ▢400 g (15 oz) Black beans, Drained and rinsed ▢Jalapeños, To taste

Instructions Chop everything and add to the bowl. Make the dressing but leave in a separate small bowl or jug. Add just before serving and mix well. Sprinkle tortilla chips and feta on top.
